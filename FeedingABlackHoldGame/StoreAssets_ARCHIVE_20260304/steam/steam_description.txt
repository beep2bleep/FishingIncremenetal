Title: Feeding A Black Hole

Short description:
Fling planets to fuel a growing black hole — an arcade-incremental with upgrades, chaos, and cosmic charm.

Full description:
Feed the void and master gravity in "Feeding A Black Hole" — a compact incremental-action game where you aim, fling, and strategize to grow an unstoppable black hole. Collect mass, buy upgrades, and trigger rare cosmic events that alter gameplay and reward cunning players.

Key Features:
- Mix of aim-based fling mechanics and incremental progression
- Deep upgrade tree with branching playstyles
- Random events and rare objects for high replayability
- Minimalist art with satisfying visual and audio feedback
