# Vanguard - Idle Auto Battle

## Long Description (Master)
Vanguard - Idle Auto Battle is a progression-first auto battler where the fight runs continuously and your decisions shape the outcome.

As your squad advances, enemy pressure rises from routine waves into demanding boss phases. You will recruit specialized heroes, tune their stats, and decide when to spend power on active abilities that can swing a run.

Every run feeds permanent growth. Better upgrades unlock stronger economy, cleaner survivability, and faster clears, creating the classic "one more run" loop with visible momentum.

### Core Experience
- Auto battles with real frontline pressure and enemy pacing
- Four hero roles with distinct behavior and active abilities
- Power economy that rewards timing, not just passive waiting
- Boss fights with segment-based progress and payout moments
- Persistent upgrade progression across repeated runs
- Session flexibility with speed control support

### Why It Feels Good
- Frequent reward events keep momentum high
- Build changes are immediately visible in combat performance
- Active abilities add tactical peaks to an idle-friendly loop
- Upgrades affect offense, defense, economy, and uptime, enabling many growth paths

### Player Fantasy
You are not micromanaging every attack. You are commanding the whole combat economy: when to invest, when to burst, and how to convert one run into the next major power jump.
