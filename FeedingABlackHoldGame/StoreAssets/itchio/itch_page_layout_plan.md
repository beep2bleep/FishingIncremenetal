itch.io Page Layout Plan

Top of page:
- Strong short hook (1 sentence)
- 1 hero screenshot or short gameplay GIF

Immediately below:
- 3 to 6 bullet features

Middle section:
- Core loop explanation (5-step sequence)
- 3 to 5 additional screenshots focused on action and progression

Lower section:
- Controls
- Session length expectations
- Current status and update notes

Footer:
- Contact / community links

Screenshot shot list:
1) Combat with full squad visible
2) Active ability visual moment
3) Boss segment pressure moment
4) Upgrade tree / progression screen
5) Reward spike / post-battle summary
