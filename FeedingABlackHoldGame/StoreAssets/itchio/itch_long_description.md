# Vanguard - Idle Auto Battle

Your team fights automatically. You control the growth curve.

Vanguard - Idle Auto Battle blends idle progression with tactical auto combat. Build your squad, trigger hero actives at key moments, and convert every run into permanent upgrades that push your next run further.

## Why Play
- Real auto battle pacing with frontline pressure
- Four heroes with distinct roles and active abilities
- Boss fights that reward timing and build quality
- Persistent progression across offense, defense, and economy
- Fast feedback loops with long-term optimization depth

## Core Loop
1. Enter battle and let your squad engage automatically.
2. Manage power and trigger hero actives at high-value moments.
3. Clear waves, pressure boss segments, and collect rewards.
4. Spend earnings on upgrades that change future runs.
5. Repeat with stronger builds and faster clears.

## Gameplay Features
- Auto battler structure with strategic interventions
- Upgrade-driven scaling and unlock chains
- Distinct hero synergies and build paths
- Reward events that create momentum spikes
- Single-player progression designed for repeat sessions

## Session Style
Playable in short bursts or long optimization sessions. The game is built to make progress visible every run.

## Controls
- Mouse: UI, activations, and menu flow
- Core combat is automated; strategy comes from timing and upgrade choices

## Status
Actively iterated. Feedback on balance and progression pacing is welcome.
