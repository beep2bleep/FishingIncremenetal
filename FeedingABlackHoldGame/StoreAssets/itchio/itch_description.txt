Title: Feeding A Black Hole

Feed the void — fling planets, harvest mass, and grow an ever-hungry black hole in this incremental arcade experience.

Feeding A Black Hole is an incremental / clicker-action hybrid where you fling celestial bodies into a hungry black hole to increase its mass, unlock upgrades, and discover chaotic cosmic events. Fast to pick up with deep upgrade trees, emergent combos, and charming pixel-art effects.
