Vanguard - Idle Auto Battle is a progression-first strategy auto battler.

Your team battles on its own. You drive the outcome by shaping upgrades, managing power economy, and triggering active abilities at the right time.

Build a multi-hero lineup, scale through escalating waves, and break boss phases for bigger payouts. Then reinvest every run into permanent growth to accelerate the next one.

Features:
- Auto battle combat with meaningful tactical decisions
- Four heroes with distinct roles and active powers
- Boss encounters with segment-style progression pressure
- Persistent upgrades across offense, defense, and economy
- Session-friendly pacing with deep long-run optimization
- Single-player PvE progression loop built for repeat runs
